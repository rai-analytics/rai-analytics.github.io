Featured Project

FMCG Sector Pakistan 2019–2024 Panel Data Inflation Analysis

Advertising Spend During Peak Inflation: An Analysis of Market Leaders in the Pakistani FMCG Sector (2019–2024)

An independent study examining how 15 of Pakistan's top PSX-listed FMCG companies allocated advertising budgets during one of the country's most severe inflationary periods. I built a custom Python data extraction pipeline to collect structured financial data from six years of annual reports, constructed a balanced panel dataset, and applied SPSS regression to test long-held assumptions about advertising effectiveness — in the Pakistani context specifically.

15 PSX-Listed Firms
6 Years (2019–24)
90 Observations
PSX Data Source

Key Finding

Advertising expenditure showed no statistically significant effect on gross profit margin during Pakistan's 2019–2024 inflationary cycle. Firms exhibited "advertising rigidity" — maintaining budgets regardless of macro pressure — a finding that directly challenges conventional FMCG marketing assumptions.
