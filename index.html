FMCG Sector Pakistan 2019–2024 Panel Data Inflation Analysis

Advertising Spend During Peak Inflation: An Analysis of Market Leaders in the Pakistani FMCG Sector (2019–2024)

An independent study examining how 15 of Pakistan's top PSX-listed FMCG companies allocated advertising budgets during one of the country's most severe inflationary periods. I built a Python data-extraction pipeline using AI-assisted parsing (Gemini API) to collect structured financial data from six years of annual reports, constructed a balanced panel dataset, and applied SPSS regression to test long-held assumptions about advertising effectiveness.

Key Finding

Advertising expenditure showed no statistically significant effect on gross profit margin during Pakistan's 2019–2024 inflationary cycle. Firms exhibited "advertising rigidity" — maintaining budgets regardless of macro pressure — a finding that directly challenges conventional FMCG marketing assumptions.

Business Implication

FMCG firms should consider reallocating rigid advertising budgets toward targeted price-promotions and trade marketing during inflationary periods, where measurable ROI is more directly achievable.
